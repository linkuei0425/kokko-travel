Airport Guide

(1) Okinawa Monorail (Yui Rail)

  • Best for City Center: Domestic Terminal 2F connects directly to the station. To 【Kenchomae】 (Kokusai Dori) ~12 mins, 【Omoromachi】 (New City Center) ~19 mins.
  • Note: From International Arrivals, follow the signs and walk about 5 mins to reach the Domestic Terminal and Monorail connection.
Buy Monorail Pass

(2) Limousine Bus (Resort Direct)

  • Pros: Direct buses designed for resort hotels in the Central and Northern areas (Onna, Nago). Avoids transfer and luggage hassle.

(3) Car Rental Shuttle / Taxi

  • Car Rental: Most agencies provide shuttle buses outside the airport. Boarding at Stops 11-14 outside Domestic Terminal 1F.
  • Taxi: ~1,500 - 2,000 JPY to Kokusai Dori. Great value for group travelers.
