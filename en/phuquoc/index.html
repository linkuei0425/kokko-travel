Phu Quoc Airport (PQC) Guide

(1) VinBus Free Shuttle

  • Pros: Exclusive to Phu Quoc! Free AC bus between the airport and Grand World/Vinpearl.
  • Schedule: Fixed departures. Best budget choice, but takes longer.

(2) Grab Ride Hailing

  • Pros: Usable island-wide. Enter your hotel name directly to avoid price haggling and scams.
  • Pick-up point: Cross the street after exiting the terminal to the designated ride-hailing area.

(3) Klook Private Transfer

  • Pros: Name-sign pickup. Highly recommended for red-eye flights, large luggage, or families. Safe and direct to hotel.
Book Phu Quoc Airport Transfer

Setup & Registration

  • Download the Grab App and register with your phone number before departure.
  • Link a credit card (overseas cashback cards recommended) for direct payment in Vietnam, avoiding cash hassle.
  • Confirm the exact pick-up point (green dot) on the App when booking. Send a photo to the driver if needed.
