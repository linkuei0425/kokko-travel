Regional Highlights

Travel Tip: Public transport in Phu Quoc mainly relies on Grab or private cars. For the North Island, downloading the VinBus App is highly recommended to check real-time schedules for free electric buses like Route 17 (Airport to Grand World) and Route 19 (Central/South to Grand World). The climate is warm year-round; please remember sun protection and hydration.
