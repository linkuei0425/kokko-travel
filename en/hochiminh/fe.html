About this page - Ho Chi Minh City (Saigon) Travel Guide

This is a curated Ho Chi Minh City attractions guide provided by Kokko Travel / Im+ Life. The content is divided into "Historic Sites" and "Trendy Spots". Historic sites cover District 1's Saigon Central Post Office, Independence Palace, and Notre-Dame Cathedral, as well as District 3's War Remnants Museum. Trendy spots feature The Cafe Apartment, Thao Dien Creative District, and the Pink Church (Tan Dinh). The guide highly recommends using the newly opened MRT Line 1 and Grab for transportation.
