※ Public transport in Da Nang and Hoi An relies mainly on Grab, buses, and tourist shuttles. Download the Danabus App for city buses. For traveling between Da Nang and Hoi An, take the LK02 Bus or book a shared shuttle via Klook/KKday. It's warm year-round, so remember to wear sunscreen and stay hydrated.
