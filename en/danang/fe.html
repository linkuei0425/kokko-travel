About this Page

This page is the ultimate Kokko Travel guide for Da Nang and Hoi An attractions. It categorizes top tourist spots into four main regions:

  • Da Nang City: Including Dragon Bridge, Han Market, and Pink Church.
  • Son Tra Peninsula: Featuring My Khe Beach, Lady Buddha, and hidden gems.
  • Ba Na Hills: Featuring the Golden Bridge, French Village, and Alpine Coaster.
  • Hoi An: Featuring the Ancient Town, Japanese Bridge, and Night Market.

Each spot includes detailed features and transit instructions (Grab, Danabus, etc.).

※ Public transport in Da Nang and Hoi An mainly relies on Grab, buses, and tourist shuttles. We highly recommend downloading the Danabus App for city buses.
