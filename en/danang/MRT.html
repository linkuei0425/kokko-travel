Da Nang & Hoi An Public Transport Guide

This is a smart routing application by Kokko Travel. It helps users calculate the fastest routes, transfers, and travel times using local transport networks including:

  • Danabus Route D12 (Coastal Tourism Line)
  • Inter-provincial Bus LK02 (Da Nang to Hoi An)
  • Airport Shuttles (DAD Airport to City)
  • Hoi An Local Shuttles/Trams
  • Ba Na Hills Shuttles

Use the dropdowns below to select a starting point and destination to generate a step-by-step travel itinerary with nearby attractions.
