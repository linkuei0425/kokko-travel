Airport Guide

(1) Grab / Gojek

  • Pros: DAD airport is very close to the city center. Using Grab provides cheap and transparent pricing.
  • Duration: Only 10-15 minutes to city center or My Khe Beach.
  • Pickup: Walk out of the International Terminal and follow the signs to the designated ride-hailing pickup area.

(2) Traditional Taxi

  • There are many taxis available outside. We recommend choosing reputable brands like Vinasun or Mai Linh, and always ask them to go "By Meter".

(3) Private Transfer

  • Pros: The most stress-free option. A driver will wait with a sign. Highly recommended for late arrivals or heading directly to Hoi An.
Book DAD Airport Transfer

Register & Bind Card

  • Please download the Grab App and complete phone registration in your home country before departure.
  • Bind your credit card (preferably one without foreign transaction fees) so you can ride cashless upon arrival.

Beware of Fake Drivers

  • Important: At the arrival hall, people might approach you claiming to be your Grab driver.
  • Ignore them! Always check your app and verify the "License Plate" and "Driver Photo" before getting into any car.
