Da Nang Airport Guide

(1) Grab / Gojek Ride-hailing

  • Advantage: The airport is extremely close to the city. Grab is transparent in pricing and very cheap.
  • Duration: Only 10-15 mins to the city center (e.g., Dragon Bridge) or My Khe Beach.
  • Pick-up: Walk out of the international terminal and follow signs to the designated ride-hailing pick-up point.

(2) Traditional Taxi

  • There are many taxis outside. It is recommended to choose reputable brands like Vinasun or Mai Linh and insist on the meter.

(3) Klook Private Transfer

  • Advantage: The most premium and stress-free option. Meet-and-greet service, perfect for late-night arrivals or heading straight to Hoi An.

Register & Bind Card Early

  • Please download the Grab App and register your phone number in your home country first.
  • Bind a credit card (preferably one with no foreign transaction fees) to use cashless payment upon arrival.

Beware of Fake Drivers

  • Important: Many people in the arrivals hall might approach you claiming to be Grab drivers.
  • Ignore them! Look at your app and verify the license plate and driver's photo before getting in.
