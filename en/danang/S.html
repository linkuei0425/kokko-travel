Da Nang & Hoi An AI Itinerary Planner

This is an intelligent itinerary generator built by Kokko Travel. Users can select attractions across Da Nang City, My Khe Beach, Son Tra Peninsula, Ba Na Hills, and Hoi An Ancient Town. The application's algorithm then calculates transport times, optimal routing, and time-window constraints to build a personalized 3 to 7-day trip schedule. It includes booking links and transit instructions.
