Seoul Airport Guide

(1) AREX Airport Express

  • Express Train: Non-stop to Seoul Station. ~43 mins from T1. Reserved seats. Best for Seoul Station/Myeongdong travelers.
  • All-Stop Train: Stops at every station. Transfer to Line 2 at Hongik Univ. Station. Best for Hongdae/Sinchon travelers.
Buy AREX Express Ticket

(2) Airport Limousine Bus

  • Pros: Routes all over Seoul (e.g., 6001 to Myeongdong, 6002 to Hongdae). Direct access to major hotels without carrying luggage through subway stairs.
  • Payment: Use T-money card or buy tickets at airport booths.
Book Airport Bus Ticket

(3) Private Airport Transfer

  • Pros: Premium door-to-door service. Ideal for family groups, red-eye flights, or heavy luggage.
Book Private Transfer

(1) Subway Transfer

  • Line 5: Direct to Yeouido, Gwanghwamun, Dongdaemun.
  • Line 9: Best for Gangnam area. Includes both Express and Local trains.
  • AREX: Quick link to Hongik Univ. and Seoul Station.
