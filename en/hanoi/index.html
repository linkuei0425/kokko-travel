Noi Bai Airport (HAN) Guide

(1) Express Airport Bus 86

  • Pros: Designed for travelers, spacious and clean, stops at major locations around the Old Quarter.
  • Fare: Approx. 45,000 VND. Purchase tickets directly on board.
  • Time: About 50-60 minutes to Hoan Kiem Lake.

(2) Grab Ride-Hailing

  • Pros: Available 24/7, drops you off directly at your hotel doorstep.
  • Pick-up: Cross the street after exiting the terminal to reach the designated ride-hailing area.

(3) Klook Private Transfer

  • Pros: Premium and stress-free. The driver waits at the arrival hall with a name sign. Ideal for late flights or groups.
