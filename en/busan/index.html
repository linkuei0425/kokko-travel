HCMC Airport Guide

(1) Grab / Gojek Ride-Hailing

  • Pros: Transparent pricing, no scams or detours.
  • Time: 30-45 mins to District 1 (City Center).
  • Pickup: Look for designated Grab pillars outside International Arrivals.

(2) Airport Bus 152 / 109

  • Bus 152: Cheapest option (~5k VND) to Ben Thanh Market. Luggage may cost extra.
  • Bus 109: Yellow tourist bus, spacious for luggage, stops at major hotels.

(3) Klook Private Transfer

  • Pros: Most comfortable and stress-free. Driver meets you with a sign. Great for late arrivals or families.
Book SGN Airport Private Transfer

Register & Add Card in Advance

  • Download the Grab App and register your phone number before arriving in Vietnam.
  • Link a credit card (preferably with no foreign transaction fees) for cashless rides.

Beware of Fake Drivers

  • Important: Ignore people approaching you in the arrivals hall claiming to be Grab drivers.
  • Do not engage! Follow the App's directions to the pickup point and check the license plate before getting in.
