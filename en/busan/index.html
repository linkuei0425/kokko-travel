Busan Airport & KTX Guide

Korea eSIM WOWPASS Airport Transfer

(1) Light Rail + Metro

  • Route: Take the Gimhae Light Rail from the airport to [Sasang Station] for Metro Line 2, or to [Daejeo Station] for Metro Line 3.
  • Pros: Cheapest option and avoids traffic jams.

(2) Airport Limo & Bus

  • Limo Bus: Comfortable direct rides to hotels, mainly covering "Seomyeon/Busan Station" and "Haeundae" routes.
  • City Bus: If traveling light, Bus 307 goes directly to Haeundae.

(3) Taxi

  • Best For: Groups of 3-4 people or travelers with heavy luggage.
  • Estimated Fare: Around 15k-20k KRW to Seomyeon; 25k-30k KRW to Haeundae.
