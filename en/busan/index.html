Airport Guide

Korea eSIM WOWPASS Airport Transfer

(1) Gimhae Light Rail + Metro

  • Route: Take the Light Rail from the airport to [Sasang Station] to transfer to Metro Line 2 (towards Seomyeon/Haeundae), or to [Daejeo Station] to transfer to Metro Line 3 to head downtown.
  • Pros: Cheapest fare and traffic-free (Light rail is around 1,300 KRW, transit discount available with transit card).

(2) Limousine & City Bus

  • Airport Limousine: Comfortable and directly reaches major hotels. Two main routes: "Seomyeon/Busan Station" and "Haeundae". Fare: 6,000 - 8,500 KRW.
  • City Bus: If traveling light, take Bus 307 directly to Haeundae for standard bus fare.

(3) Taxi

  • Features: Great for groups of 3-4 or carrying large luggage. Standard (silver/white) and deluxe (black) taxis are outside the terminal.
  • Est. Fare: To Seomyeon around 15,000 ~ 20,000 KRW; to Haeundae around 25,000 ~ 30,000 KRW (Nighttime/traffic surcharges apply).
