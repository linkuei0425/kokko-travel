🇰🇷 Busan & Surrounds Tour

Busan 5 Days • Yeongdo • Gyeongju

Day 1: Arrival & Old City Charm

Daytime

Arrive at Gimhae International Airport and head to your hotel to drop off luggage (Seomyeon or Nampo-dong recommended). In the afternoon, stroll around the Nampo-dong shopping district, visit Yongdusan Park, or head to Jagalchi Market to taste fresh sashimi and seafood.

Transport: Take the Light Rail from Gimhae Airport to Sasang Station and transfer to Subway; Yongdusan Park is near Subway Line 1 "Nampo Station"; Jagalchi Market is near Subway Line 1 "Jagalchi Station".

Airport Private Transfer Airport City Shuttle Bus

Evening

Head to BIFF Square to taste the famous Ssiat Hotteok (seed sweet pancake), and stroll through Gukje Market to feel the bustling local atmosphere.

Transport: Subway Line 1 "Jagalchi Station" Exit 7, short walk to BIFF Square. Gukje Market is just across the street.

Night

Go up Busan Tower to overlook the charming Busan port and city night view, then enjoy authentic Dwaeji Gukbap (pork soup rice) nearby.

Transport: Located in Yongdusan Park. You can take the free outdoor escalator from Gwangbok-ro shopping street.

Must Buy! Visit Busan Pass

Day 2: Fairy Tale Village & Marine Cable Car

Morning

Head to Gamcheon Culture Village. Change into Hanbok, wander through the colorful stepped alleys, find the Little Prince statue, and take stunning photos.

Transport: Subway Line 1 "Toseong Station" Exit 6, transfer to green mini-bus (Saha 1-1, Seo-gu 2, Seo-gu 2-2) to Gamcheon Culture Village.

Book Gamcheon Hanbok Experience

Afternoon

Head to Songdo Beach. Walk on the Songdo Skywalk, then take the Songdo Marine Cable Car across the bay and enjoy the thrilling ride in a transparent crystal cabin.

Transport: Subway Line 1 "Nampo Station" Exit 8, transfer to city bus (No. 6, 30, 71) to Songdo Beach.

Night

Enjoy grilled hagfish or Korean BBQ for dinner, then head to Bupyeong Kkangtong Market for late-night snacks.

Transport: Subway Line 1 "Jagalchi Station" Exit 3 or 5, walk about 10 mins to Kkangtong Market.

Day 3: Romantic Haeundae & Sky Capsule

Daytime

Head to Haeundae Blueline Park. Take the popular Sky Capsule from Mipo to Cheongsapo, enjoy the magnificent coastline along the way, and take photos at the Cheongsapo railroad crossing, resembling the scene from "Slam Dunk".

Transport: Subway Line 2 "Jungdong Station" Exit 7, walk about 15 mins to "Mipo Station". From Cheongsapo, you can take a mini-bus back.

Haeundae Sky Capsule Ticket

Night

Stroll along Haeundae Beach, then head to The Bay 101 to capture romantic water reflections against the skyscraper backdrop, enjoying a luxurious city night.

Transport: Subway Line 2 "Dongbaek Station" Exit 1, walk 10 mins to The Bay 101; "Haeundae Station" Exit 3 or 5, direct to the beach.

Day 4: Gijang Coast & Lotte World

Morning

Head to Gijang-gun in East Busan. First, visit Haedong Yonggungsa, Korea's most beautiful seaside temple, and pray while listening to waves crashing against the rocks.

Transport: Donghae Line "OSIRIA Station", transfer to bus (No. 139, 1001) or take a 5-min taxi ride.

Afternoon

Head to Lotte World Adventure Busan to experience thrilling rides, or go to the nearby Skyline Luge to enjoy the speed of downhill karting.

Transport: Donghae Line "OSIRIA Station" Exit 1, walk about 15 mins to the park and Luge.

Lotte World Adventure Busan Ticket

Night

Highly recommend heading to Gijang Market for dinner. Pick a fresh snow crab and enjoy a highly cost-effective steamed crab feast!

Transport: Donghae Line "Gijang Station" Exit 1, walk about 5 mins.

Day 5: Seomyeon Shopping & Departure

Morning

Wake up naturally and head to Seomyeon. This is Busan's busiest shopping paradise. Explore the underground mall and visit the hipster Jeonpo Cafe Street for a pour-over coffee.

Transport: Subway Lines 1 & 2 intersect at "Seomyeon Station"; Jeonpo Cafe Street is near Subway Line 2 "Jeonpo Station" Exit 7.

Afternoon

Do some last-minute souvenir shopping (seaweed, Korean instant noodles, snacks) at Lotte Department Store or hypermarkets (Lotte Mart / Homeplus), then head to the airport for your flight home.

Transport: Seomyeon Station connects directly to Lotte Dept Store. To the airport, go to Subway Line 2 "Sasang Station" and transfer to Gimhae Light Rail to "Airport Station".

Yeongdo is an island connected to Nampo-dong, full of rustic fishing village charm and stunning hidden coastal views.

⚓ Yeongdo & Huinnyeoul Culture 1-Day Tour

Morning

Take a bus to Taejongdae. Ride the Danubi train to the observatory and lighthouse, enjoying the dense pine forest and magnificent cliff ocean views. On a clear day, you can even see Japan's Tsushima Island.

Transport: Subway Line 1 "Nampo Station" Exit 6, transfer to bus (No. 8, 30, 66, 88, 186) to "Taejongdae" terminal stop.

Afternoon

Visit Huinnyeoul Culture Village, known as the "Santorini of Busan". Walk along the coastal cliff path and explore independent bookstores, craft shops, and ocean-view cafes.

Transport: Subway Line 1 "Nampo Station" Exit 6, transfer to bus (No. 6, 7, 9, 71, 82, 85) and get off at Baengnyeonsa or Yeongdo Catholic Church stop.

Night

Return to the Yeongdo Bridge area. Visit the Samjin Amook History Museum to buy authentic fish cakes, then experience Korean local street food culture at the Pojangmacha (tented food stalls) next to Yeongdo Bridge.

Transport: Yeongdo Bridge is near Nampo Station, reachable by foot; Pojangmacha are mostly gathered along the Nampo-dong port.

🏛️ Gyeongju Millennium Capital 1-Day Tour

Morning

Take the KTX or bus from Busan to Gyeongju. First stop: Bulguksa Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site, to feel the solemn beauty of Korean Buddhist architecture (great for autumn foliage and spring cherry blossoms).

Transport: Take KTX from Busan Station to "Singyeongju Station" and transfer to Bus 700; or take intercity bus from Nopo Station to Gyeongju, transfer to Bus 10/11 to Bulguksa.

From Busan: Gyeongju Historic Capital Day Tour

Afternoon

Head to Daereungwon to see the massive ancient tombs of Silla kings. Then stroll down Hwangridan-gil right next to it, filled with trendy cafes, restaurants, and craft shops renovated from Hanoks. Perfect for wearing Hanbok for photos.

Transport: Take Bus 10 or 11 from Bulguksa back to the city, get off at "Daereungwon" or the bus terminal, walk to Daereungwon and Hwangridan-gil.

Evening / Night

At sunset, head to Donggung Palace and Wolji Pond. This was the banquet venue for Silla royalty. After dark, the lights illuminate the palace and lake, creating breathtaking reflections. Afterwards, take a bus back to Busan.

Transport: About 15-20 mins walk from Daereungwon, or take a city bus. To return to Busan, take a bus from the terminal or KTX from Singyeongju Station.
