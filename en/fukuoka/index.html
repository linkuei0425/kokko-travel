Airport Guide

(1) Fukuoka Subway (Recommended)

  • Convenience: The station is directly under the Domestic Terminal. Only 2 stops (5 mins) to [Hakata], and about 11 mins to [Tenjin]!
  • Note: After arriving at the International Terminal, you must take the free shuttle bus (10-15 mins) to the Domestic Terminal to transfer to the subway.
Buy Subway 1-Day Pass

(2) Nishitetsu Bus (Direct)

  • Pros: Board directly outside the International Terminal. No need to take the shuttle bus. Direct access to Hakata, Tenjin, and Dazaifu.

(3) Taxi

  • Features: The airport is very close to the city. A taxi to Hakata Station costs about 1,500 JPY, making it very cost-effective and easy if shared by 3-4 people.
