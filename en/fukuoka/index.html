Airport Guide

(1) Fukuoka Subway (Recommended)

  • Convenience: Station is directly under Domestic Terminal. 2 stops (5 mins) to [Hakata], ~11 mins to [Tenjin]!
  • Note: Arriving at Int'l Terminal, take the free shuttle bus (10-15m) to Domestic Terminal for subway transfer.
Buy Subway 1-Day Pass (Klook)

(2) Nishitetsu Bus (Direct)

  • Pros: Board outside Int'l Terminal. No shuttle needed. Direct to Hakata, Tenjin, Dazaifu.

(3) Taxi

  • Features: Very close to city center. Taxi to Hakata is ~1,500 JPY. Cost-effective if shared by 3-4 pax.
