🌸 Fukuoka In-depth Tour

City 5 Days • Dazaifu • Yufuin

Day 1: Arrival & Yatai Culture
Daytime

Arrive at Fukuoka Airport, take the subway just 2 stops to Hakata Station. After dropping off luggage at the hotel, enjoy classic "Hakata Ramen" (like Shin-Shin or Issou) around the station.

Transport: Take the Subway Kuko Line from Fukuoka Airport Domestic Terminal, just 2 stops (about 5 mins) to "Hakata Station".
Night

Head to Nakasu-Kawabata in the evening. Experience Japan's unique "Yatai" (food stall) culture along the Naka River, enjoying oden, mentaiko tamagoyaki, and draft beer.

Transport: Take the Subway Kuko or Hakozaki Line to "Nakasu-Kawabata Station", and walk about 5 mins to the riverside Yatai street.
Day 2: Tenjin Shopping & Ohori Park
Morning

Take a walk in "Ohori Park", enjoying the lakeside view and Starbucks concept store. During cherry blossom season, the nearby Maizuru Park is a perfect viewing spot.

Transport: Take the Subway Kuko Line to "Ohori Park Station", then walk about 2 mins.
Afternoon

Move to "Tenjin Underground Mall" and surrounding department stores for a shopping spree. From Japanese fashion to trendy cosmetics, you'll return fully loaded.

Transport: Take the Subway Kuko Line to "Tenjin Station" or Nanakuma Line to "Tenjin-minami Station", which directly connects to the underground mall.
Night

For dinner, try Fukuoka's must-eat "Motsunabe" (beef tripe hot pot). Rich white miso soup base with plump beef tripe, perfectly finished with champon noodles.

Transport: Famous Motsunabe restaurants (like Echigoya or Oyama) are easily accessible on foot around Tenjin or Hakata Station.
Day 3: Seaside Momochi & Fukuoka Tower
Afternoon

Take a bus to Seaside Momochi. Visit MARK IS mall next to PayPay Dome and experience thrilling rides at Boss E・ZO Fukuoka.

Transport: Take Nishitetsu Bus (e.g., route 306, W1) from "Hakata Station" or "Tenjin Station" and get off at "PayPay Dome" or "Kyushu Medical Center".
Night

Climb "Fukuoka Tower" at dusk to enjoy a romantic sunset and stunning night views of Seaside Momochi.

Transport: Walk about 10-15 mins from PayPay Dome or MARK IS to Fukuoka Tower. Take a Nishitetsu Bus back to the city.
Dazaifu and Yanagawa are located south of Fukuoka, perfect for a one-day suburban railway trip.
🌸 Dazaifu & Yanagawa 1-Day Tour
Morning: Dazaifu

Take Nishitetsu from Tenjin to Dazaifu. Visit "Dazaifu Tenmangu" (Shrine of Learning), take photos at the Kengo Kuma-designed Starbucks, and don't forget to try fresh-baked "Umegaemochi" on the approach.

Transport: Take Tenjin Omuta Line from "Nishitetsu Fukuoka (Tenjin) Station" to "Nishitetsu Futsukaichi Station", then transfer to Dazaifu Line to "Dazaifu Station". Direct buses are also available from Hakata Bus Terminal.
Afternoon: Yanagawa

Continue via Nishitetsu to the water town "Yanagawa". Experience a romantic river punting tour while listening to the boatman's traditional songs. For dinner, try the local specialty "Steamed Eel Rice".

Transport: Return to "Nishitetsu Futsukaichi Station" from "Dazaifu", then take Tenjin Omuta Line (Ltd. Exp. ~35 mins) to "Nishitetsu Yanagawa Station". Transfer to a free shuttle or local bus to the boarding point.
Yufuin is a famous hot spring town in Oita Prefecture, known for the morning mist of Lake Kinrin and fairy-tale village vibes.
♨️ Sightseeing Train Yufuin no Mori
Transport & Experience

Take the green-painted sightseeing train "Yufuin no Mori" from Hakata Station. Stroll along "Yunotsubo Street", enjoy B-speak roll cakes and gold-award croquettes, and capture the fairy-tale reflections at "Lake Kinrin".

Transport: Take the Ltd. Exp. "Yufuin no Mori" directly from JR "Hakata Station" to JR "Yufuin Station" (approx. 2h 15m, all reserved seats require advance booking). Highway buses from Hakata Bus Terminal are also an option.
