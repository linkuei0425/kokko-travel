Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) about Bangkok Food

Where can I find Michelin-recommended street food in Bangkok?
You can find legendary Michelin street food like Jay Fai (Crab Omelet) in the Old Town, Go-Ang Pratunam Chicken Rice in the Siam area, and Charoen Saeng Silom (Pork Knuckle) in the Silom area.
Which are the best rooftop bars with food in Bangkok?
Top recommendations include Red Sky at Centara Grand, Tichuca in Sukhumvit, Sky Bar at Lebua (Riverside), and Mahanakhon Bangkok SkyBar.
Where is the best place for traditional Thai desserts?
The Old Town is famous for Kor Panit's Mango Sticky Rice, while After You Dessert Cafe offers modern Thai tea shaved ice across multiple branches.
