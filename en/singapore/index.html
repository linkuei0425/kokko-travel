Changi Airport Guide (SIN)

(1) MRT

  • Route Info: Station is located between T2 and T3. Take the Green Line (CG Branch) and alight at "Tanah Merah" station. Cross the platform to transfer towards the city.
  • Payment: Use Apple Pay/Google Pay or a contactless card directly at the gantry (SimplyGo).
Singapore NETS FlashPay Card

(2) Ride-hailing & Private Transfers

  • Ride-hailing: Follow signs to "Ride Hailing" zones. Fare to the city is ~$20-30 SGD (20-30 mins).
  • Private Transfers: Highly recommended for families or lots of luggage.
Book Private Airport Transfer

Jewel Changi Guide

  • Location: In front of T1 Arrival Hall. Walkable via bridges from T2/T3.
  • Rain Vortex: World's tallest indoor waterfall. Don't miss the free light show at night.
  • Early Check-in: Drop bags early at Jewel (L1) to shop hands-free.

Multicultural Districts

Singapore is a multi-ethnic melting pot. Step into these cultural enclaves and feel like you've traveled to another country. Great for exotic vibes and authentic food.

📍 Chinatown

Traditional meets Modern

Visit the Buddha Tooth Relic Temple, try Tian Tian Chicken Rice at Maxwell Food Centre, and grab a cocktail at Ann Siang Hill at night.

MRT Blue/Purple Line "Chinatown"

📍 Little India

Colorful Spices & Garlands

Visit the Sri Veeramakaliamman Temple, shop at the 24/7 Mustafa Centre for affordable souvenirs, and enjoy authentic prata.

MRT Blue/Purple Line "Little India"

📍 Kampong Glam

Arabian Charm & Hipster Alleys

Centered around the stunning Sultan Mosque. Haji Lane is full of street art, indie boutiques, and cafes, making it a youth favorite.

MRT Green/Blue Line "Bugis"
