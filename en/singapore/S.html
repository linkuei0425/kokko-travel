Singapore City 3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Jewel Changi & Marina Bay Magic
Daytime

Upon arriving at Changi Airport, head straight to Jewel Changi next door to admire the breathtaking 40-meter indoor waterfall "Rain Vortex" and enjoy Song Fa Bak Kut Teh or Shake Shack. In the afternoon, head to your city hotel to drop off luggage and rest.

Transport: Jewel Changi is located in front of Terminal 1. To get to the city, take the MRT East West Line (transfer at Tanah Merah) or take a Grab.
Jewel Changi Airport Attractions Ticket
Evening

Head to Merlion Park for iconic souvenir photos, then take a beautiful stroll along the Marina Bay waterfront promenade towards Marina Bay Sands. Enjoy dinner at the MBS food court or head to nearby Lau Pa Sat for delicious authentic satay.

Transport: Merlion Park is about a scenic 10-minute walk from Raffles Place MRT station.
Night

Watch the spectacular "Spectra" light and water show at Marina Bay Sands, then go up to the Sands SkyPark Observation Deck to overlook Singapore's magnificent million-dollar city skyline.

Day 2: Multicultural Vibes & Avatar Gardens
Morning

Explore Singapore's diverse cultures! First, head to Kampong Glam to visit the majestic Sultan Mosque and Haji Lane, full of colorful street murals. Then proceed to Little India to feel the vibrant energy, colorful architecture, and rich spices.

Transport: Kampong Glam is near Bugis MRT; Little India is located at Little India MRT.
Afternoon

Head to Chinatown to visit the impressive Buddha Tooth Relic Temple, and enjoy the famous Tian Tian Hainanese Chicken Rice at Maxwell Food Centre for lunch. In the afternoon, explore Gardens by the Bay's massive Flower Dome and Cloud Forest conservatories.

Gardens by the Bay Conservatories Ticket
Night

At 7:45 PM or 8:45 PM, find a spot on the grass at Supertree Grove and enjoy the free, dazzling Garden Rhapsody light and sound show, feeling like you are stepping into a futuristic wonderland.

Day 3: Shopping Paradise & River Cruise
Daytime

In the morning, visit the National Gallery Singapore for an inspiring cultural experience. Then, embark on a shopping adventure along Orchard Road, offering everything from accessible brands to luxury boutiques. Treat yourself to a local street-side ice cream block.

Transport: Take the MRT to Orchard or Somerset stations for the heart of the shopping district.
Evening

Head to Clarke Quay for dinner. Indulge in Singapore's national culinary pride—Chili Crab (try popular spots like Jumbo Seafood). The rich sauce paired with golden fried mantou buns is an unforgettable match!

Night

Take a relaxing Singapore River Cruise. Enjoy the gentle evening breeze while admiring the brightly lit riverside promenades, towering financial district skyscrapers, and Marina Bay landmarks.

Singapore River Cruise Ticket

Sentosa Island Itinerary

Sentosa is Singapore's premier island resort getaway, featuring Universal Studios, beautiful sandy beaches, and thrilling activities. We highly recommend dedicating a full day here.

🌴 Sentosa: Sun & Thrills 1-Day Tour
Morning

Take the Sentosa Express onto the island and head straight to Universal Studios Singapore (USS). It boasts incredibly immersive experiences like the Transformers 3D ride and thrilling roller coasters, making it perfect for all ages.

Transport: Take the MRT to HarbourFront, enter VivoCity 3rd floor, and transfer to the Sentosa Express. Alight at Resorts World Station.
Universal Studios Singapore Ticket
Afternoon

If theme parks aren't your preference, the S.E.A. Aquarium is a fantastic indoor alternative. In the late afternoon, head to Siloso Beach to experience the fun Skyline Luge or MegaZip to feel the refreshing ocean breeze.

Transport: Take the Sentosa Express to the terminal Beach Station.
Skyline Luge Ticket MegaZip Adventure Ticket
Evening / Night

Order a refreshing fresh coconut or mocktail at a beachside cafe and watch the sunset. Don't miss the spectacular Wings of Time night show on the beach—its dazzling water screens and fireworks conclude the day perfectly.

Mandai Wildlife Reserve Itinerary

The Mandai Wildlife Reserve features world-class open-concept environments. It's an absolute must-visit for families and nature lovers seeking an eco-educational experience.

🦁 Mandai Wildlife Reserve 1-Day Tour
Afternoon (Recommended Start)

Since the park is located away from the city center, arriving in the early afternoon is ideal. Visit the Singapore Zoo or River Wonders first. The parks use innovative hidden barriers, allowing you to observe incredible wildlife like manatees and capybaras up close in naturalistic habitats.

Transport: Take the MRT North South (Red) Line to Khatib Station, then transfer to the Mandai Khatib Shuttle which goes straight to the parks (approx. 15 mins).
River Wonders Singapore Ticket
Night

As night falls, the main event begins! Enter the world's first Night Safari. Take the guided tram ride through the dimly lit jungle to observe the fascinating nocturnal behaviors of lions, elephants, and tigers. It is an incredibly unique educational adventure.

Singapore Night Safari Ticket

Cross-Border Day Trips

Singapore serves as a fantastic travel hub. Taking a short trip to Johor Bahru (Malaysia) or the islands of Bintan and Batam (Indonesia) offers diverse cultural experiences and great value!

🇲🇾 Johor Bahru (JB) Family 1-Day Tour
Morning

Prepare your passport and MDAC (Malaysia Digital Arrival Card) for an exciting overland border crossing! After clearing customs, head straight to Legoland Malaysia, Asia's first Legoland theme park—a wonderland for families and children.

Transport: Take a cross-border bus from Woodlands or the KTM Shuttle Train (a rapid 5-min journey across the causeway). For Legoland, booking a direct chartered bus is highly recommended for convenience.
Legoland Ticket Return Bus Pass Legoland Hotel
Afternoon

If you prefer shopping, JB is an incredible retail and culinary haven. Visit Johor Premium Outlets (JPO) for discounted brands, or enjoy affordable local snacks, trendy cafes, and relaxing family-friendly hair salons around City Square.

Evening / Night

It's best to head back before the late evening rush hour to avoid Causeway traffic. Grab a quick local dinner or light supper before returning comfortably to your Singapore hotel.

🇮🇩 Indonesia Bintan 3-Day Resort Escape

Day 1: Departure & Private Beach Relaxation
Morning

Bring your passport to the ferry terminal and sail across the beautiful strait to Bintan, Indonesia. Upon arrival, check into a stunning family-friendly resort (such as Natra Bintan or Club Med).

Transport: Ferries depart from Singapore's Tanah Merah Ferry Terminal. The scenic boat ride takes roughly 60 minutes.
Singapore-Bintan Ferry Natra Bintan Resort
Afternoon

Change into your swimwear and dive into the resort's massive infinity pool or relax on the private sandy beach. Unwind completely in this tropical paradise.

Night

Enjoy a wonderful family dinner featuring local Indonesian cuisine at the resort, falling asleep to the peaceful, gentle sound of ocean waves.

Day 2: Mangrove Adventure & Water Sports
Morning

Embark on a fascinating Mangrove Discovery Tour. Cruise on a small boat through dense mangrove forests to safely spot wild monkeys, exotic birds, and rich biodiversity.

Bintan Mangrove Tour
Afternoon

Head to Treasure Bay, featuring Southeast Asia's largest man-made seawater lagoon. Families and kids will love the endless activities ranging from slip-and-slides to clear kayaks.

Treasure Bay Water Activities
Night

If the night is clear, consider joining a magical Firefly Night Tour to witness nature's glowing wonders lighting up the quiet mangroves—an unforgettable sight for children.

Day 3: Family Spa & Departure
Morning

Enjoy a slow morning. After a hearty buffet breakfast, book a traditional Indonesian family-friendly wellness spa session to completely rejuvenate before heading home.

Afternoon

Check out and pick up some beautiful Indonesian handicrafts or local treats at the terminal shops. Finally, board the ferry back to Singapore, wrapping up a perfect island escape.

🇮🇩 Batam Seafood & Wellness 1-Day Tour
Morning

Grab your passport and hop on a fast ferry to Batam, Indonesia! Famous for its incredibly budget-friendly shopping and dining, it's a favorite weekend backyard for locals. Upon arrival, explore the Nagoya Hill Mall or charter a car for a comfortable island sightseeing tour.

Transport: Ferries primarily depart from Singapore's HarbourFront Centre. The journey takes a quick 45-60 minutes.
Premium Batam 1-Day Tour Batam Private Car Charter
Afternoon

Treat the family to an affordable, relaxing traditional Indonesian wellness spa. Afterward, a visit to a local "Kelong" (over-water restaurant) is mandatory! Enjoy a massive, fresh seafood feast at unbeatable prices—golden prawns and sweet crab are highly recommended.

Batam Spa Central Package
Evening / Night

Don't forget to purchase Batam's famous signature souvenir—Kueh Lapis (traditional layered cake)—before catching your ferry back to the bright lights of Singapore.
