City 3 Days • Sentosa • Mandai Wildlife • Cross-Border

Day 1: Jewel Changi & Marina Bay Glamorous Night
Daytime

Upon arriving at Changi Airport, head straight to Jewel Changi next door to admire the 40-meter indoor waterfall "Rain Vortex" and enjoy Song Fa Bak Kut Teh or Shake Shack. In the afternoon, head to your city hotel to drop off luggage and rest.

Transport: Jewel Changi is located in front of T1; to get to the city, take the MRT Green Line (transfer at Tanah Merah) or take a Grab.
Jewel Changi Airport Attractions Ticket
Evening

Head to Merlion Park for iconic photos and take a stroll along the Marina Bay waterfront to Marina Bay Sands. Have dinner at the MBS food court or head to nearby Lau Pa Sat for satay.

Transport: Merlion Park is about a 10-minute walk from Raffles Place MRT station.
Night

Watch the Spectra light and water show at Marina Bay Sands, then go up to the Sands SkyPark Observation Deck to overlook Singapore's million-dollar night view.

Day 2: Multicultural Vibes & Avatar Gardens
Morning

Explore Singapore's diverse cultures! First, head to Kampong Glam to visit the Sultan Mosque and Haji Lane, full of colorful murals. Then proceed to Little India to feel the strong aroma of spices and colorful architecture.

Transport: Kampong Glam is near Bugis MRT; Little India is located at Little India MRT.
Afternoon

Head to Chinatown to visit the Buddha Tooth Relic Temple, and have Tian Tian Hainanese Chicken Rice at Maxwell Food Centre for lunch. In the afternoon, head to Gardens by the Bay to visit the huge Flower Dome and Cloud Forest conservatories.

Gardens by the Bay Conservatories Ticket
Night

At 7:45 PM or 8:45 PM, lie on the grass at Supertree Grove and enjoy the free, dazzling Garden Rhapsody light show, feeling like you are in an Avatar movie scene.

Day 3: Shopping Paradise & Clarke Quay Cruise
Daytime

In the morning, visit the National Gallery Singapore for some art vibes. Then, embark on a shopping spree along Orchard Road, offering everything from affordable brands to luxury boutiques. Rest with a cup of Bacha Coffee or a street-side ice cream block.

Transport: Take the MRT to Orchard or Somerset stations for the Orchard Road shopping district.
Evening

Head to Clarke Quay. Have Singapore's national dish "Chili Crab" (like Jumbo Seafood or No Signboard Seafood) for dinner. The sauce paired with fried mantou is a perfect match!

Night

Take a Singapore River Cruise, enjoy the breeze, and admire the brightly lit bar streets, financial district skyscrapers, and Marina Bay night views, experiencing the prosperity of the Asian Tiger.

Singapore River Cruise Ticket
Sentosa is Singapore's holiday paradise, featuring Universal Studios, beautiful beaches, and rich amusement facilities. We recommend dedicating a full day.
🌴 Sentosa: Sun & Thrills 1-Day Tour
Morning

Take the Sentosa Express onto the island. Go straight to Universal Studios Singapore (USS). Although small, it has unique attractions like the Transformers 3D ride and the world's tallest dueling roller coaster, suitable for all ages.

Transport: Take the MRT to HarbourFront, enter VivoCity 3rd floor, and transfer to Sentosa Express to Resorts World Station.
Universal Studios Singapore Ticket
Afternoon

If theme parks aren't your thing, head to the S.E.A. Aquarium to escape the heat. In the afternoon, head to Siloso Beach and experience the thrilling Skyline Luge or MegaZip to feel the ocean breeze and speed.

Transport: Take the Sentosa Express to the terminal Beach Station.
Skyline Luge Singapore Ticket Mega Adventure Park (MegaZip) Ticket
Evening / Night

Order a cocktail at a beachside bar to watch the sunset. Don't miss the Wings of Time night show on the beach; the spectacular fireworks and lasers conclude the Sentosa trip perfectly.

🦁 Mandai Wildlife Reserve Family 1-Day Tour
Afternoon (Recommended)

Since the park is far from the city, arriving in the afternoon is recommended. Visit the Singapore Zoo or River Wonders first. The parks use hidden barriers, allowing you to observe orangutans, manatees, and capybaras up close.

Transport: Take the MRT Red Line to Khatib, transfer to the Mandai Khatib Shuttle directly to the park (approx. 15 mins).
River Wonders Singapore Ticket
Night

As night falls, the main event begins! Enter the world's first Night Safari. Take the guided tram ride through the dark jungle to observe the nocturnal lives of lions, elephants, and tigers. An incredibly unique experience.

Singapore Night Safari Ticket
Singapore has convenient transport to surrounding areas. Heading to Johor Bahru (JB), Bintan, or Batam to experience exotic cultures and affordable prices is a very popular choice!
🇲🇾 Malaysia Johor Bahru (JB) 1-Day Tour
Morning

Prepare your passport and MDAC (Malaysia Digital Arrival Card), and experience overland cross-border travel! After clearing customs, head straight to Legoland Malaysia, Asia's first Legoland theme park, perfect for families.

Transport: Take a cross-border bus from Woodlands or the KTM Shuttle Train (only 5 mins across the bridge, but tickets sell out fast). For Legoland, booking a direct chartered car or bus is highly recommended.
Legoland Malaysia Ticket Singapore - Legoland Return Bus Pass LEGOLAND Malaysia Resort Hotel
Afternoon

If you prefer shopping over theme parks, JB is a shopping and food paradise. Head to Johor Premium Outlets (JPO) for a shopping spree, or enjoy affordable Vietnamese hair washing, massages, authentic Bak Kut Teh, and Malaysian coffee around City Square.

Evening / Night

Avoid the evening rush hour traffic jam and return to Singapore via the Causeway. Have dinner near your hotel with some light food or supper.

🇮🇩 Indonesia Bintan 3-Day Resort Tour
Day 1: Departure & Private Beach Relaxation
Morning

Bring your passport to the ferry terminal and sail across the Strait of Malacca to Bintan, Indonesia. Upon arrival, check in to your booked luxury resort (e.g., Banyan Tree or Club Med).

Transport: Take a ferry from Singapore's Tanah Merah Ferry Terminal. The boat ride takes about 60 minutes to reach Bintan.
Singapore - Bintan Ferry Ticket Natra Bintan, a Tribute Portfolio Resort
Afternoon

Change into your swimsuit and enjoy the private beach, tropical sun, and infinity pool at the resort. Relax completely and enjoy an undisturbed, leisurely time.

Night

Have a romantic Nanyang-style dinner at the resort or book a beach BBQ, and fall asleep to the gentle sound of the waves.

Day 2: Mangrove Adventure & Water Sports
Morning

Join the unique "Mangrove Tour," taking a small boat through the dense mangrove forests to spot wild monkeys, snakes, and kingfishers, experiencing raw nature.

Bintan Mangrove Tour
Afternoon

Head to Treasure Bay water park and experience Southeast Asia's largest man-made seawater lagoon. You can enjoy wakeboarding, ATVs, or clear kayaks, perfect for outdoor lovers.

Treasure Bay Bintan Water Activities
Night

If it's a clear, moonless night, join a "Firefly Night Tour" to admire the dancing fireflies in the quiet mangroves, feeling like a fairy tale.

Day 3: Indonesian Spa & Departure
Morning

Enjoy a morning without alarms. After a hearty buffet breakfast, book an authentic Indonesian traditional Spa massage to completely wash away the fatigue of the past few days.

Afternoon

Check out and buy some Indonesian handicrafts or local snacks at shops around the terminal. Then, take the ferry back to Singapore, concluding your relaxing island getaway.

🇮🇩 Indonesia Batam Seafood & Massage 1-Day Tour
Morning

Set off with your passport! Take a ferry to Batam, Indonesia, where prices are incredibly friendly—it's Singaporeans' favorite weekend backyard. Upon arrival, shop around Nagoya Hill Mall or charter a car for an island tour.

Transport: Take a ferry from Singapore's HarbourFront Centre. The ride takes about 45-60 minutes.
<From Singapore> Premium Batam 1-Day Tour Batam Private Car Charter (by JE Travel)
Afternoon

Experience a super affordable and comfortable traditional Indonesian massage (Spa) to relax your body and mind! Afterward, you must go to a local Kelong water restaurant for a cheap and huge seafood feast. We recommend golden prawns and black pepper crab.

Batam Spa Central Package
Evening / Night

Buy some of the most famous local handmade layer cake (Kueh Lapis) or cheap snacks as souvenirs, and take the ferry back to Singapore.
