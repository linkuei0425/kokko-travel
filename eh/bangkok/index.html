Airport Guide

Premium Airport Transfer

(1) Airport Rail Link (ARL)

  • Location: Basement B1 Floor.
  • Time: ~26-30 mins to Phaya Thai terminus.
  • Transfer:
    • Makkasan (A6): Transfer to MRT Blue Line (Phetchaburi).
    • Phaya Thai (A8): Transfer to BTS Sukhumvit Line.
  • Hours: 05:30 – 24:00 (every 10-15 mins).

(2) Public Taxi

  • Location: 1st Floor outside Gates 4 & 7. Get a ticket from the queue machine.
  • Fare: Metered fare + 50 THB airport surcharge + highway tolls (~75 THB), total approx. 350-500 THB.

(3) Ride-Hailing (Grab / Bolt)

  • Location: Wait at designated pick-up points (usually 1st Floor, outside Gates 4 or 7).
  • Pros: Transparent pricing, can request specific vehicle types.

(4) LimoBus Express

  • Route: Two routes available: Khao San Road and Silom/Siam area.
  • Fare: Approx. 150 THB, direct to downtown.
Premium Airport Transfer

(1) SRT Red Line

  • Location: 10-15 mins walk following airport signs.
  • Route: Airport → Krung Thep Aphiwat Central Terminal (Bang Sue).
  • Fare/Time: Approx. 33 THB, 17-20 mins ride.
  • Transfer: Transfer to MRT Blue Line at Central Terminal for city center.

(2) Airport Bus (A1-A4)

— Best for BTS transfers

  • Location: Terminal 1 Gate 6 or Terminal 2 Gate 12.
  • A1 (30 THB): To BTS Mo Chit / MRT Chatuchak, every 5 mins. (Recommended)
  • A2 (30 THB): To Victory Monument.
  • A4 (60 THB): To Khao San Road.

(3) Public Taxi

  • Location: Dedicated taxi stand outside Arrivals.
  • Fare: Metered fare + 50 THB surcharge + tolls, total approx. 250-400 THB.
