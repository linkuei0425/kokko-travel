Airport Guide

Super e-SIM Airport Transfer

(1) Airport Taxi

  • Location: Outside Arrival Hall Exit 1.
  • Cost: Fixed rate to Old City / Nimman is usually 150 THB. Large cars are ~200 THB.
  • Features: No meter needed, transparent pricing. Great if you have large luggage.

(2) Ride-hailing (Grab/Bolt/Maxim)

  • Location: Designated pick-up points outside the Arrival Hall.
  • Cost: Varies by distance & time, usually 100 - 180 THB to the city. Bolt/Maxim are generally cheaper than Grab.

(3) RTC City Bus

  • Routes: Take the Green Line to Chiang Mai Gate, Tha Phae Gate, or Warorot Market.
  • Cost: Flat rate 30 THB per ride.
  • Features: Air-conditioned, cheapest option, but less frequent.

(1) Chiang Rai Airport Taxi

  • Location: Outside the Arrival Hall.
  • Cost: Fixed rate to city center (near Clock Tower) is ~200 THB.

(2) To Chiang Mai City

— Requires intercity bus transfer

  • Method: Take a taxi to Chiang Rai Bus Terminal (1 or 2), then take the Green Bus to Chiang Mai.
  • Duration: Chiang Rai to Chiang Mai takes about 3 - 3.5 hours by bus.
