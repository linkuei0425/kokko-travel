Home
Day 1: Arrival & Old City Charm
Daytime

Arrive at Chiang Mai Airport, head to your hotel to drop off luggage. In the afternoon, stroll around the Old City. Visit Tha Phae Gate to feed the pigeons, and explore the ancient and majestic Wat Chedi Luang.

DIY Transport: Airport Taxi (~150 THB) or Grab/Bolt to city. Inside Old City is mostly walkable, or take a Red Songthaew (30 THB/ride) for short distances.
Evening

Head to the Nimman Road area. Wander around One Nimman to experience the artistic and hipster vibe of Northern Thailand.

DIY Transport: Take a Grab, Bolt, or Tuk-Tuk to Nimman area (~80-120 THB from Old City).
Night

If it's the weekend, visiting the Chiang Mai Weekend Markets (Saturday at South Gate / Sunday at Tha Phae Gate) is highly recommended. On weekdays, head to the Chiang Mai Night Bazaar to try Northern Thai sausages and banana roti.

DIY Transport: Walk or take a Red Songthaew (30 THB) to the respective night markets depending on the day.
Day 2: Doi Suthep & Khantoke Dinner
Morning

Take a songthaew or private car to Doi Suthep. Visit the magnificent Wat Phra That Doi Suthep and enjoy a panoramic view of Chiang Mai city from the viewpoint.

DIY Transport: Red Songthaew from Chiang Mai University or North Gate (~60-80 THB/way, waits to fill up). Alternatively, charter one for ~600 THB round trip.
Afternoon

After descending the mountain, choose a jungle-style or highly Instagrammable cafe (like Chom Cafe and Restaurant or No.39 Cafe) for a relaxing afternoon tea.

DIY Transport: Use Grab or Bolt for convenient point-to-point travel from the mountain base to cafes.
Night

Experience a traditional Khantoke Dinner. Enjoy Khao Soi (Northern Thai curry noodles) while watching traditional Northern Thai dance performances.

Book Old Chiang Mai Cultural Center Khantoke Dinner!
DIY Transport: Grab or Bolt is the easiest way to reach the cultural center or restaurant.
Day 3: Ethical Elephant Experience
Daytime

Join a half-day or full-day Elephant Sanctuary tour. No riding involved! Change into farmer's clothes, hand-make rice balls to feed the elephants, and give them a mud bath. It will definitely be an unforgettable memory.

Popular! Elephant Jungle Sanctuary Half Day Tour
DIY Transport: Almost all sanctuary tours include free round-trip hotel transfers. No transport worries today!
Night

After returning to the city and freshening up, head to the Old City's North Gate (Chang Phuak Gate) to try the famous "Cowboy Hat Lady Pork Leg Rice" (Khao Kha Moo). Then, enjoy an authentic traditional Thai massage.

Fah Lanna Spa Massage Experience (Old City Branch)
DIY Transport: Red Songthaew (30 THB) or Grab/Bolt to the North Gate for dinner and spa.
Day 4: Cooking Class & Art Village
Morning

Sign up for a Thai Cooking School. Follow the instructor to the local market to buy fresh ingredients, and learn how to cook Tom Yum Goong, Green Curry, and Mango Sticky Rice. Enjoy your own creations for lunch!

Fun Thai Cooking Class (No Experience Needed)!
DIY Transport: Cooking classes usually include free hotel pickup and drop-off.
Afternoon

Visit Baan Kang Wat. It is a creative village full of greenery where you can experience pottery making, painting, or just grab a coffee in a quiet independent bookstore.

DIY Transport: Located slightly out of town. Highly recommend taking a Grab or Bolt (~100-150 THB from Old City).
Night

Head to the Mae Ping River, pick a riverside restaurant (such as The Good View) for a romantic dinner, and listen to a live band performance.

DIY Transport: Grab/Bolt is the safest and most convenient option to travel at night.
Day 5: Local Markets & Souvenirs
Morning

Head to Warorot Market, the largest traditional market in Chiang Mai. Shop for local Northern Thai souvenirs: crispy pork rinds, green curry paste, northern spices, and various dried fruits.

DIY Transport: Take a Red Songthaew (30 THB) or walk if you are near Tha Phae Gate.
Afternoon

Do some last-minute shopping at Maya Lifestyle Shopping Center before heading to the airport, or preparing to depart for nearby cities (Chiang Rai, Pai).

DIY Transport: Grab/Bolt (~150-200 THB) from Maya Mall or your hotel to the airport. Plan to leave 2.5 hours early due to potential traffic.

🏛️ Chiang Rai Iconic Temples 1-Day Tour
Morning

Depart from Chiang Mai with a short stop at the Mae Khachan Hot Springs for a foot soak. Upon arrival, first visit the majestic and pure White Temple (Wat Rong Khun), a contemporary art masterpiece resembling heaven.

White, Blue & Black Temple Day Tour (from Chiang Mai)
DIY Transport: Take the "Green Bus" from Chiang Mai Arcade Bus Terminal 3 (~3.5h, ~200-300 THB). Recommend booking tickets in advance via their App.
Afternoon

Next, explore the vibrantly colored Blue Temple (Wat Rong Suea Ten), followed by a visit to the mysterious and bizarre Baan Dam Museum (Black House).

DIY Transport: In Chiang Rai, attractions are far apart. Renting a scooter or using Grab/Bolt between temples is required (Booking a day tour is often easier).
Evening / Night

Optional visit to the Long Neck Karen Village to learn about the local ethnic minority culture, before taking the bus back to Chiang Mai city.

DIY Transport: Return via Green Bus, or relax on your comfortable day tour bus back to your Chiang Mai hotel.
About a 2-hour drive from Chiang Mai. The highest peak in Thailand, featuring cool weather and beautiful scenery.
🏔️ Doi Inthanon National Park 1-Day Tour
Morning

Head to Doi Inthanon National Park. Reach the highest point monument in Thailand to enjoy the forest breeze. Visit the magnificent Twin Royal Pagodas, surrounded by beautiful gardens and views of the sea of clouds.

Book Doi Inthanon National Park Day Tour!
DIY Transport: Public transport is very difficult. You must charter a Red Songthaew/Private Taxi for the whole day (~2,500-3,500 THB) or join a tour.
Afternoon

Visit a Karen Tribe village and the Royal Project agricultural area to taste locally grown high-mountain Arabica coffee. On the way back, admire the powerful Wachirathan Waterfall.

DIY Transport: Self-drive (car/scooter) is possible but only recommended for experienced drivers due to very steep and winding roads.
About a 3-hour drive from Chiang Mai (via 762 curves). A mountain town full of bohemian vibes.
Pai Adventure 1-Day Tour
Morning

Get ready to experience an exciting adventure with an ATV ride, jungle ziplining, and white water rafting.

DIY Transport: Chiang Mai to Pai: Take a Minivan (Prempracha Transport) from Chiang Mai Arcade Bus Terminal 2 (~3h, ~150 THB). Be prepared for 762 curves!
Afternoon

Visit the unique Bua Tong Waterfall (Sticky Waterfall), where you can climb up the limestone rocks directly through the cascading water.

Sticky Waterfall, ATV, Zipline & Rafting Adventure
DIY Transport: In Pai, there is almost no public transport. Renting a scooter (~150-250 THB/day) is the absolute best way to get around.
